Overview

The Provide First Aid course is the most comprehensive first aid qualification available. Delivered over one intensive day, this nationally recognised course equips you with the skills and knowledge to respond confidently to medical emergencies in any setting.

Whether you're a healthcare professional, trainer, or want to build your emergency response capabilities, this course meets industry standards and is accepted across Australia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course nationally recognised?
Yes. The Provide First Aid course is nationally recognized and meets the requirements of major Australian first aid standards. Your certificate is valid for 3 years and accepted across all Australian states and territories.
Do I get my certificate on the same day?
Absolutely. Upon successful completion of the course and passing the assessment, you'll receive your nationally recognised certificate on the same day.
What if I have no prior first aid experience?
No prior experience is necessary. Our trainers accommodate all levels and ensure everyone completes the course with confidence. We start from the basics and progress through to advanced techniques.
How long is the certificate valid?
Your Provide First Aid certificate is valid for 3 years from the date of issue. We recommend refresher training before renewal to stay current with the latest techniques.
Is this course NDIS compliant?
Yes, this course is NDIS compliant and can often be funded through NDIS support packages. Please contact us for more information about funding options specific to your circumstances.
Can I enrol if I have medical conditions or mobility limitations?
We welcome all participants and can provide reasonable adjustments. Please discuss your specific needs with us during enrollment so we can ensure the course is accessible and suitable for you.
