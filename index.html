About Mark Institute

Empowering the Future of Healthcare Excellence

Mark Institute Australia is a premier, Perth based training provider established to address the vital need for advanced clinical skills in the NDIS, aged care, and disability sectors. We were founded on the principle that the highest standard of care is only achievable through specialized, practical education.

By bridging the gap between basic support and high intensity clinical mastery, we have set a new benchmark for workforce development and professional excellence in Western Australia.

To empower the next generation of healthcare professionals, Mark Institute Australia provides a comprehensive curriculum of over 26 nationally compliant courses. We specialize in complex support training including Peg Feeding, Catheter Care, and Seizure Management ensuring that our students gain the practical skills, confidence, and accredited qualifications required to thrive in Australia's evolving healthcare landscape.
